Featured Programs

A selection of programs we think you’ll like

Digital Business Management

This 1-year study + 1 year co-op program will equip you with specialist skills and industry relevant knowledge in business, digital marketing and cybersecurity for a successful career in business management in the new digital economy.

Find Out More

Digital Marketing

Gain a qualification in digital marketing to create innovative campaigns to transform your business. This program is powered by Direct Marketing Institute. Upon successful completion, you will also receive a Digital Associate Certificate.

Find Out More

Information Technology

Getting an IT diploma especially in the field of cybersecurity opens up your future to a whole host of opportunities around the world. This program is powered by CompTIA, giving you the knowledge to build a solid foundation towards the Security+ certification.

Find Out More

Big Data

Choosing to study within this area will serve you well as you develop concrete, in-demand data analytics skills. The Diploma in Data Analytics Co-op is powered by Amazon Web Services Educate and Tableau, helping you to gain the knowledge to optimize your organizational performance.

Find Out More

EnglishPlus+ is an initiative that allows intermediate students to join the Languages Canada accredited English for Academic Purposes (EAP) Program, providing a strong foundation for students and creating a direct pathway into TSoM and Niagara College – Toronto (NCT) programs.

Find Out More

Accounting

Skilled accountants are always in demand and gaining a globally-recognized qualification is a huge bonus. We are an ALP Gold provider for our Preparatory Course for ACCA Examination, demonstrating our high performance standards. 

Find Out More

Business

Getting a diploma in business opens up a variety of different careers in a range of organizations. You will learn how to use Quickbooks software for various bookkeeping purposes.

Find Out More

Hospitality and Tourism

Learning how to succeed in hospitality and tourism is a good choice for those who want to work anywhere in the world.  You will learn how to use the Oracle Hospitality and OPERA Property Management Cloud Services – the same software that’s used in actual hotels.

Find Out More

Paging

 

* Students will be offered paid or unpaid entry-level positions related to their field of studies. The Career Services Department will provide full support to students on booking and preparing for interviews. It is the student’s responsibility to perform well during all interviews as well as during the full length of the co-op term. Placements are subject to availability and will vary based on the program, season and job market changes as well as the student’s English level and previous professional and academic experience. Should the co-op placement not be available the student will be required to complete a Capstone Project as an alternative to graduate from the program.

Neelam Sharma
Neelam originated from India and pursued international education at TSoM, graduating and acquiring the Diploma in Cybersecurity Specialist Co-op. She found a program with co-op, which helped her gain relevant skills, as she was able to apply all that she learned from class in her current job.

Leticia Santos
A dreamer, achiever, and believer, Leticia never stopped setting goals that helped her improve her career. She recently acquired her Diploma in Digital Marketing Specialist Co-op and plans to level up by furthering her studies.

Melisa Garzon Flores
As a civil engineer back home, she decided to pursue the Diploma in Business Administration Program to learn more about project management, start a new business, and level up her career.

Cheryl Ranganadhan
She is currently studying the Preparatory Course in the ACCA Examination and recently achieved the highest mark in the Audit and Assurance (AA) paper in North America. She also placed eighth globally for the same paper. With a determined attitude, she was able to achieve this milestone - something she least expected due to the busy nature of her work as a Director of Travel Trade at one of the famous hotels in Canada.

Lucas De Souza Campos
As an international student, the life experiences Lucas learned from the time he first set foot in Toronto, until he finished his studies, made him fall in love with the city more. He is now planning to stay here and help the Canadian market grow. He aspires to contribute significantly to the growth of Canada by applying all the knowledge and skills he obtained from studying at TSoM.
